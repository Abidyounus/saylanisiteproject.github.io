Welcome To Saylani Welfare Non-Government Organization In Pakistan

"The Largest NGO Offering Free Assistance To Needy People"

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a prominent non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Karachi, Pakistan, established in May 1999 by Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui. The organization is dedicated to alleviating poverty and improving the lives of underprivileged individuals across Pakistan and beyond.
